The situation

The product was fully built and deployed behind a registration gate, four deployment steps from going live. Six workstreams were unresolved and blocking commercial launch. There was no formal tracking, no prioritisation, and no documentation. A three-week window was set to resolve all of it.

My role

IT Program Manager. Brought in to impose structure on a solo-founder product, drive the launch critical path, coordinate across six parallel workstreams, and build the documentation and operational foundation the product needed to move from built to live.

What I delivered

A record of work produced across this engagement. Documents contain confidential client information and are available privately on request.

  • 30-day PM onboarding plan structured into four phases, built around low founder availability
  • Project-level RAID log covering risks, assumptions, issues, and dependencies from the project brief
  • Team and resourcing RAID log covering gaps in the current team structure and what they block
  • Three-week prioritised Gantt chart with six workstreams, owners, and day-level granularity
  • Competitor pricing research across five platforms with verified current pricing
  • Recommended Breachrr paid tier structure with monthly and annual pricing
  • UK company registration research and step-by-step documentation enabling Stripe and Paddle access
  • Nigeria startup outreach list with 10 targeted prospects across direct and MSP channel categories
  • All stakeholder communications including post-call briefs, scoping emails, and project plan cover emails
